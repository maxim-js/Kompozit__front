Why is the hydraulic floor warm

Energy
saving up to 30%

The room is heated from floor to ceiling – you don't waste extra energy on heating upstairs. The integrated temperature sensors help you save even more.

Temperature
control

Temperature settings are different for each room – you can control them in automatic or manual mode. mode. So the house is not too cold or too hot.

Service life
over 50 years

The main heating element of the system is a cross-linked polyethylene pipe, it can serve up to 50 years. of years. To ensure that the warm floors last as long as possible, we will give advice on how to use them.

Uniform heating
of the room

The warm water floor heats up the entire area on which the floor is laid – surface and air temperature in the floor. the room is the same, no cold spots. Feel as comfortable as possible.

Easy
to repair

The equipment requiring maintenance and repair is located in a separate room – no need for a master. to enter a residential area. The heating unit is quickly dismantled for troubleshooting.

Large
energy reserve

The concrete screed of a warm floor keeps warm for a long time when the system is switched off. Even if you stay without electricity due to a power failure, the house will be comfortable.

Does not
dry air

When using a water warm floor, the minimum air drying is achieved, because it is sincerely not comes into contact with the heating element.

Minimum
dust movement

The air is not stirred when using warm floors, the heating is uniform. That's why dusts less than when using radiators.

Aesthetic
view

Furniture, decor and indoor plants can be freely placed near the windows – nothing interferes. Interior turns out to be more modern and stylish.

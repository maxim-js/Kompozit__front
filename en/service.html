Hydraulic

The hydraulic warm floor system is a floor-mounted pipe system that contains warm water. This heating system is distinguished by the fact that the air is heated all over the place areas of the floor. The heated air is at the bottom, and when it starts to rise, it cools down. At the same time premise warm and not stuffy. Hydraulic warm floor system is safe and economical, creating more healthy air atmosphere than other types of heating.
